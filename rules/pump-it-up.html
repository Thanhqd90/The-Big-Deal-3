General Rules

 

  • Bar use is allowed at all times.
  • All speed mods and noteskins are allowed.
  • Mods from the Display category are allowed.
  • Mods in the Path, Alternate, and Rush categories are not allowed.
  • BGA DARK is to be used at all times unless agreed by both players.
  • All arbitration situations will be handled at the tournament coordinator’s discretion.

 

**If at any point the tournaments start to fall behind, there may be changes to the format (example, omit doubles charts for certain rounds)**

 

If you indicate that you concede (example stepping off the pad completely, verbally announcing you give up), then your opponent wins the song

 

Per Round1 policy, shirts and shoes are required at all times.

 

If at any point you do something deemed unsportsmanlike, such as verbally/physically interfering with someone while playing a match, damage Round 1 property, become visibly intoxicated, assault another person, or any other similar malicious behavior, you will be immediately disqualified from the tournament, kicked out of the venue, and will not receive a refund.

 

Disqualification

 

Players who fail to report to their matches after following criteria are met will be immediately given an automatic match loss.  

 

  • Player absence hinders tournament progress  
  • Player is not in another match for a different game
  • Player fails to communicate with staff on their whereabouts

 

 

Players may also be given warnings for unsportsmanlike conduct and and excessively stalling during their match which can lead to a DQ if player repeats offense.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Formats

 

Knockout format -

All players in the group play the same 2 songs.

Top _x_ advance based the cumulative scores of the songs.  

 

Double Elimination 

Must lose twice to be out of the tournament.

 

Seeding incentives 

Before each match, players will choose from the following options in alternating order.  Higher seed gets first choice.

 

  • Player 1 Side or player 2 side
  • First turn or second turn in card draw.
  • Play first or second in doubles rounds (If applicable).

 

Card draw format 

A predetermined number of charts are randomized.  

Each player protects 1 song followed by vetoing charts in alternating order until one song remains.

 

Songs will be played in the order they were protected followed by the remaining song as the tie breaker.

 

**If both players protect a doubles chart, the remaining doubles charts will be vetoed and continue with the remaining singles charts**

Travel Information

Round 1 Bowling and Amusement (Grapevine Mills Mall)

Conveniently located north of I-635 and west of the 121 toll road on the southwest corner of Grapevine Mills Mall. For specific directions, please use the address:

3000 Grapevine Mills Pkwy Grapevine, TX 76051


  • Dallas-Fort Worth International Airport (DFW)

    Closest to the venue and most accessible from all major domestic and international airports, DFW is the central airport for the area. Most of the hotels by the mall also offer free shuttle services both to and from the airport. Airlines servicing this airport may be found here: https://www.dfwairport.com/airlines/index.php

  • Dallas Love Field Airport (DAL)

    Located in Dallas proper, this airport is a good alternative for those flying in on Southwest Airlines, (which includes two free checked bags, as well as two free carry on items, if you're short on space!). DAL is ~20 miles from Grapevine Mills Mall, and you can't get there easily by public transit; if you fly into DAL, please plan your transportation accordingly! Airlines servicing this airport may be found here:
    http://www.dallas-lovefield.com/air-carriers.html
