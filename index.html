The Big Deal: 3D

The largest annual multi rhythm game tournament Texas is back for its 3rd installment on March 29-31st at Round 1 in the Grapevine Mills Mall.

The Big Deal: 3D will be featuring tournaments for following games:

  • Dance Dance Revolution A
  • Pump It Up Prime 2
  • Beatmania IIDX 26: Rootage
  • Sound Voltex: Heavenly Haven
  • Dance Rush Stardom

Travel Information

Round 1 Grapevine Mills Mall

3000 Grapevine Mills Pkwy, Grapevine, TX 76051, USA

  • Dallas/Ft. Worth International Airport:

    Airport code: DFW

    DFW's main airport. Closest to the venue and most accessible from nearly every other airport in the nation/internationally. Most of the hotels next to the mall also offer free shuttle service to/from the airport.


  • Dallas Love Field:

    Airport code: DAL

    Located in Dallas proper. Good alternative airport for flying in via Southwest Airlines. it's about 30 min drive from the venue.
