Travel Information

Round 1 Bowling and Amusement (Grapevine Mills Mall)

Conveniently located north of I-635 and west of the 121 toll road on the southwest corner of Grapevine Mills Mall. For specific directions, please use the address:

3000 Grapevine Mills Pkwy Grapevine, TX 76051


  • Dallas-Fort Worth International Airport (DFW)

    Closest to the venue and most accessible from all major domestic and international airports, DFW is the central airport for the area. Most of the hotels by the mall also offer free shuttle services both to and from the airport. Airlines servicing this airport may be found here: https://www.dfwairport.com/airlines/index.php

  • Dallas Love Field Airport (DAL)

    Located in Dallas proper, this airport is a good alternative for those flying in on Southwest Airlines, (which includes two free checked bags, as well as two free carry on items, if you're short on space!). DAL is ~20 miles from Grapevine Mills Mall, and you can't get there easily by public transit; if you fly into DAL, please plan your transportation accordingly! Airlines servicing this airport may be found here:
    http://www.dallas-lovefield.com/air-carriers.html
